Module 6: Final Project: From Page to Screen

For my final project, I decided to expand upon the Blender and 3D rendering module and create a 3D rendered image from a screenplay I wrote the previous semester called Eyes Will Roll. I decided to blend a low-poly, early 2000's aesthetic with body horror, bringing to life the scene where the main character's eye comes alive and becomes sentient.
